Introduction back to ToC

The Key Performance Indicator Ontology aims to model the metrics defined at the beginning of building renovation activities to monitor the conformance with typical requirements related to energy efficient buildings. The conceptualization proposed also focuses on the relation between KPIs, projects and scenarios.
