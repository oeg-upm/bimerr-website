Introduction back to ToC

The Materials ontology is part of the ontology network developed for the H2020 BIMERR Project. The purpose of this ontology is the description of building components and materials based on their properties. The building elements and properties included in this model support the design of building renovation projects focused on energy efficiency. For the construction of the building components taxonomy, the concepts defined on the Building module of the BIMERR Network are reused. The materials taxonomy and properties are based on three well known databases in the sector of sustainable and energy-efficient buildings: Okobaudat, Baubook, and ASHRAE. Finally, the structure for the association of features of interest, properties and their values follows the SAREF Ontology
