Introduction back to ToC

BIMERR Weather Ontology has the aim of be the basis for enabling the use of BIMERR Ontology in the weather domain.

BIMERR Weather Ontology is an OWL-DL ontology that extends BIMERR Ontology with 24 classes, 27 object properties, 2 data properties and 93 individuals.
