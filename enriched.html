Enriched Ontologies

This section provides a list of the ontologies enriched with additional annotation properties in order to support the transformation to the data model required by the BIMERR Interoperability Framework. The BO2DM web service takes this models and generate the appropiate transformation, for more information take a look at the API's documentation.
